NeedNoodle

Description

We neednoodle especially high fiber instant noodle

Ingredients

  1. 1 packet of high fiber Instant noodle
  2. 2 Eggs
  3. Leafy and non leafy vegetables

Steps

  • Get a high fiber instant noodle
  • Boil 250ml water
  • Cook the noodle with boiling water
  • Add in 2 whole beaten eggs
  • Add in nicely cut vegetables
  • Enjoy the noodle
